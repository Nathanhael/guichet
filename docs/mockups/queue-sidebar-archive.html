Trade-offs
· A — Footer link: discovery OK (archive is near the team count), zero header space cost. Slightly mixes "team panel" and "mode switch" semantics in the footer bar.
· B — Title toggle link: smallest diff, mode label is single source of truth, archive stays 1 click away. No semantic mixing. My pick.
· C — Icon-only: most compact. Loses the text label, relies on tooltip for discoverability. Harder for new support agents to find archive.

All three remove the redundant equal-weight tab row, keep the dept chips in the same spot, and have no functional effect on the queue list or footer.
